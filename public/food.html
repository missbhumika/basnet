Best Food In Bhutan

Top 3 must try bhutanese dishes recommended by Bhomeka

Chicken chilli

Chilli chicken is a popular Indo-chinese appetizer made by tossing fried chicken in spicy hot chilli sauce. Learn to make the best at home.

Ema datshi

Ema Datshi, the national dish of Bhutan is a deliciously spicy stew made of chili peppers, onions and locally produced yak cheese.

Kewa datshi

Kewa Datshi, literally meaning potatoes and cheese, is a traditional Bhutanese dish made with chiles and garlic that is a vegetarian stew.
